About

3 years. 30+ websites. I care about what ranks and what converts.

I got into SEO because I wanted to understand why some websites grow without spending on ads and others stay invisible. Three years later, I have worked across e-commerce, services, travel, and B2B — and the answer is almost always the same: most sites have more to fix than to build from scratch.

At DX Creativ, I manage SEO across 7 live websites at once — technical audits, content clusters, schema implementation, and link-building quality control, all running in parallel. I also oversee 2 new website builds, making sure SEO is designed in from day one, not added as an afterthought.

"Most SEO problems are not complicated. They are a missed redirect here, thin content there, and a site structure that makes sense to the business but not to Google. I find the gaps, prioritise what moves the needle fastest, and fix them in order."

I also run Google and Meta Ads — not as a side skill, but because understanding paid and organic together makes both sharper. When I know which keywords convert in ads, I use that data to strengthen the SEO strategy.
